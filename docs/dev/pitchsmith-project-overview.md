# Pitchsmith Project Overview

Pitchsmith is an AI-powered sales simulation and pitch training platform that transforms onboarding into real-world practice.

Instead of reading static playbooks or memorizing scripts, sales teams can rehearse live conversations against AI personas, from executives and technical teams to difficult buyers and procurement committees, using voice or chat in realistic multi-stakeholder scenarios.

Pitchsmith instantly analyzes communication style, product knowledge, objection handling, confidence, and strategy while grounding every response on your company’s real documents, pricing, and sales playbooks through enterprise-grade AI knowledge retrieval.

The result is faster onboarding, more consistent messaging, stronger sales confidence, and teams that are prepared before the real meeting even begins.

Pitchsmith does not just train salespeople.
It forges them.

## Product Positioning

Pitchsmith is positioned as a sales enablement and simulation platform for teams that need faster ramp-up, better message consistency, and measurable coaching outcomes.

## Core Product Pillars

1. **AI Sales Simulation**  
   Sales teams practice realistic voice or chat conversations with AI personas and Meeting Rooms.

2. **Persona and Meeting Room Management**  
   Managers can create customer personas, define objection styles, and group multiple stakeholders into one Meeting Room for a Senario session.

3. **Playbook-Grounded Responses**  
   AI responses should be grounded in approved company playbooks, pricing, product information, and policy documents.

4. **Performance Analysis**  
   Each session is scored across communication style, product knowledge, objection handling, confidence, strategy, and closing quality.

5. **Coaching and Progress Tracking**  
   Past sessions are stored so salespeople and managers can compare progress over time through score trends and competency charts.

## Target Users

| Role | Goal |
|---|---|
| Sales Representative | Practice before real customer meetings and improve pitch confidence |
| Sales Manager | Coach consistently and identify skill gaps faster |
| Enablement/Admin | Manage playbooks, personas, rubrics, and onboarding standards |

## MVP Focus

The MVP should prioritize:

- voice Senario with single and multi-persona sessions
- persona management and Meeting Room presets
- Ask/Playbook chat grounded in approved company knowledge
- recording review with batch-based attempts and rubric scoring
- quality review for audio and document batches
- session history, score comparison, and coaching recommendations
